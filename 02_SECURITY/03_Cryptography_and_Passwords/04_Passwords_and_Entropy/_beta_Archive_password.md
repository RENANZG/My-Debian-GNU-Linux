
<h3>Create a strong password to protect your archive</h3>

<h4>Your password should be :</h4>
<ol>
            <li> At minimum 14 characters</li>
            <li> One or more of each of the following:</li>
    <ul> 
    <li>lower-case letter</li>
    <li>upper-case letter</li>
    <li>number</li>
    <li>punctuation mark or special caracter</li>
    </ul>
</ol>

<h4>You password should <b>NOT</b>:</h4>
<ul>
<li>Contain words you can find in the dictionary</li>
<li>Be the same as a passwords shown as "example strong passwords."</li>
<li>Contain personal information, such as names, birth dates…</li>
<li>Contain keyboard patterns, such as “qwerty” or “12345”</li>
<li>Contain sequences of numbers in order</li>
<li>Contain common acronyms</li>
<li>Contain repeating characters, such as mmmm3333</li>
<li>Be the same password you use for another application.</li>
</ul>
